# Sample Conversations

## Hindi / Hinglish Promise-to-Pay
Agent: Namaste Ramesh ji, main aapke two-wheeler loan EMI ke sambandh mein call kar raha hoon. Aapki INR 4,850 EMI 7 din se overdue hai. Kya aap aaj payment kar sakte hain?
Borrower: Salary abhi nahi aayi. Friday ko payment kar dunga.
Agent: Samajh gaya. Main note kar raha hoon ki aap Friday ko INR 4,850 pay karenge. Friday morning ek reminder bhej diya jayega.

## Dispute
Borrower: Ye loan mera nahi hai. Galat call hai.
Agent: Main isko dispute ke roop mein mark kar raha hoon. Hamara human support team aapse details verify karne ke liye contact karega.
